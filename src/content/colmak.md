# Less painful typing with Colemak

Do you spend hours working on your keyboard? Got some finger or wrist pain? Well here's what I did.

I started to get pain in my fingers from typing for hours a day. My wrists just hurt. At the same time I switched to only working on my own computers. So I took 1 weekend and learned the Colmak layout.

That weeken d I spent 8 hours at day doing typing tutorials. It was slow to start. Slowly I became faster and more accurate. Ok, it took me about 3 months to get back up to speed. I did take a break for 2 weeks over christmas and basically just read. So I was slower once I came back. But I now don't have any pain when I type for hours at a time.

Now I'm quicker and more accurate then when I was typing with the old Qwerty layout. And best of all no pain while typing.
